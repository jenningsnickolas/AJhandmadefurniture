Our Custom Order Process

  1. Search

    Come up with an idea of your own or look through our products and find something you'd like to modify.

  2. Conceptualize

    Draw a rough sketch of your specifications.

  3. Collaborate

    Contact Us with your idea and we'll start a line of communication. Upon receipt of $20 we'll draw up exact blueprints and continuously modify them to your satisfaction.

  4. Quote

    After as many revisions it may take, you'll receive a quote and estimated time to build. Any payments made for blueprints can be discounted from the product cost.

  5. Order

    When you place your order a non-refundable deposit of 50% of cost due is required on build start. Upon receival of product the other 50% is immediately due, plus any additional delivery costs if applicable

  6. Our Guarantee

    Simply enjoy your product. If any natural defects occur within the first year of ownership then we'll come out and do necessary repairs at no cost to you.
