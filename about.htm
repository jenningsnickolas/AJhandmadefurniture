About AJ Handmade Furniture

"Building Inheritable Furniture"

Founded in 2013 on the simple idea that furniture can once again have a personal touch. We're comprised of carprentry aficionados that purely enjoy the idea of taking raw wood to a finish product. Our builds are made for individuals that enjoy uniqueness and imperfections, that want durable furniture, and that want customizations to fit their needs.

  • Our Values
    1. Durability

      When you buy a product with us, you purchase a product that will last years. We design to withstand the day to day wear and tear over years. Our service doesn't end at purchase because if any natural problem occurs with our products then contact us and we'll make any necessary repairs.

    2. Personal

      We offer premade product or allow you place your own personal order to fit your needs. We're dedicated to providing a seamless personal experience from blueprints to color to sealant.

    3. Simplicity

      Everything we make is made from the solid wood from your local lumberyard. There's no particle board, hallow parts, or false laminates.

  • Our Future
    1. Shippables

      Build products that are shippable assembled or allow customers to assemble without much difficulty.

    2. Reorders

      Establish a lasting positive relationship with individuals so they reorder for their next project.

    3. Growth

      Increase sales to the point that prices can be lowered due to bulk purchasing discount from supplier.
